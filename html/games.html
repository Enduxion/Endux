Snake Game
Play the classical game of snake and fruit! The main goal of this game is to get as many points or score by eating the fruit as a snake. A fruit gives you one point and WASD and arrow control are used for controlling snake. Don't hit the wall and don't hit yourself!
Shooter Game
Play the classical game of shooter and enemies! The main goal of this game is to get as many points or score by shooting enemies while avoiding being shot at. Use AD control for moving, arrow key for boost and space for shooting. Pick up powerups inorder to gain cool ability!
ORX Game
Play the classic bounce ball game. The main objective of this game is to control the ball using the "striker" given. Use WS and up and down arrow key to control the "striker".
